Admission Process

Step 1
Application Form

Fill out the Student Application Admission Form (can be found at the bottom of this page). Click submit once accomplished. A notification will be sent to your email.

Note: Telephone inquiries will not be entertained.

Step 2
Upload Documents

Scan and upload your documents via the Google Form link sent to your email. A confirmation will follow to proceed to Step 3.

Step 3
Admission Fee

Pay P800 via DragonPay (link sent in the confirmation email). Proof of payment will be emailed to you.

Step 4
Schedule Test

Choose your test schedule via Google Appointment Form. Bring your test permit for onsite or await the test link for online.

Note: Telephone inquiries will not be entertained.

Step 5
Application Status
