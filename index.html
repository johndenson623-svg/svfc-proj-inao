St. Vincent de Ferrer College of Camarin, Inc. is a private educational institution managed by a dedicated family of educators.

Objectives are as follows:

  • To provide quality yet affordable education to poor but academically equipped youth.
  • To support the government's goal of bringing all Filipinos to school.
  • To help alleviate the standard of living of the people of Camarin by providing courses/programs responsive to the demands of the community.
  • To promote a continuing interest in education as we improve the standards of living for the people of Camarin by providing the demands of an emerging industrial society.
